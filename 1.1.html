History and Evolution

Money started as barter, evolved through commodity money (like gold and silver), then to representative money, and finally to fiat money, which is government-issued currency with no intrinsic value but accepted as legal tender.

What is Money?

Money is something everyone accepts in exchange for goods and services…

Concept of Money Functions

Medium of Exchange

Facilitates trade without the need for barter, making transactions efficient and universal.

Store of Value

Allows wealth to be saved and retrieved later, assuming it retains purchasing power.

Unit of Account

Provides a standard measure to price goods and services, enabling comparison and accounting.

Value of Money

Inflation is the rate at which prices increase.

Deflation is the decline in prices.

Explanation of purchasing power, inflation, and deflation.

Key Points

Inflation decreases value.
Deflation increases value.
Purchasing power matters.
